How can we help you?

General & Billing

What are your payment terms?

For most projects, we require a 50% non-refundable deposit to begin work. The remaining 50% is due upon completion and before final file delivery or website launch.

How do I request a quote?

You can request a quote by visiting our Contact section or emailing us directly at support@desinix.co.in. Please provide as much detail as possible about your project requirements.

Poster & Print Design

What file formats will I receive?

We provide print-ready PDFs (CMYK color mode) tailored to your specific printer's requirements. We also provide high-resolution JPG/PNG versions for digital use on social media.

Can you print the posters for me?

Yes! While our primary focus is design, we offer printing services for select regions. Please mention "Print & Ship" when requesting your quote to see if your location is eligible.

Website Services

Do you handle website hosting?

We can help set up hosting for you or recommend preferred providers. If you already have hosting, we can deploy the site directly to your existing server.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. We prioritize a "Mobile-First" approach, ensuring your site looks stunning and functions perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Still need help?

Our support team is available Mon-Fri to answer any specific questions about your design project.
